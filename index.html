Team Cantarctica

A Cansat team focused on thermal imaging of Antarctic ice

We are a school group participating in the European Space Agency Cansat Competition.

This involves creating a mini satellite out of a can to record and transmit data when launched.

Our primary mission is to record temperature and pressure.

Our secondary mission is to emulate studies of Antarctic ice by taking a series of infrared images which can be used to analyse the properties and condition of ice sheets.
