Building Update 1:

Hey, We recently finished our mechanical design for our CanSat. After lots of changes, we have decided on using an aluminium support structure to support our CanSat.
In addition, we have also finalised our decisions on the parachute system. we are going to recycle as much as possible by using an old sleeping bag not just for the insulation but for the parachute canopy as well
